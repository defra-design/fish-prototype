Cancel your licence renewal

To stop your licence from renewing you’ll need to cancel your recurring card payment. Your current licence will remain valid until its expiry date.

To cancel your payment:

  • cancel your payment online up to the day before your payment date at https://www.gov.uk/ea/cancel-recurring-card-payment
  • email enquiries@environment-agency.gov.uk no later than 15 days before your payment date
  • call 03708 506 506 (8am to 6pm, Monday to Friday except public holidays) up to the day before your payment date – find out about call charges at https://www.gov.uk/call-charges

Has something changed since you bought your last licence?

Update your contact details

If your address or contact details have changed, let us know so we can update our records and send your licence to the right place.

To update your contact details:

  • email enquiries@environment-agency.gov.uk no later than 15 days before your payment date
  • call 03708 506 506 (8am to 6pm, Monday to Friday except public holidays) up to the day before your payment date – find out about call charges at https://www.gov.uk/call-charges

Change your licence type

Cancel the recurring card payment and buy a new licence if you need to:

  • change the number of rods or type of fish covered by the licence
  • change to a concessionary licence because of a disability

Terms and conditions

Read the recurring card payment terms and conditions at https://www.gov.uk/ea/fishing-licence-recurring-terms.
