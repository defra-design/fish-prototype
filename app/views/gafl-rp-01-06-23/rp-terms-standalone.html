Licence holders have the option to automatically pay for and renew a rod fishing licence when it expires.

If you consent, we can arrange for future payments to be taken from your card provider. The terms of this arrangement are that:

  • a payment will be taken immediately for your 12-month licence
  • when your licence expires, a future payment will be taken by your card provider every year to cover the cost of renewing your 12-month licence
