Rods are for use by the licence holder only and must be attended at all times.

The 2 rods coarse licence is the simplest rod licence. This will cover you for all the forms of fishing with a rod and let you fish non-migratory trout and coarse fish. It will allow you to fish with 1 or 2 rods at the same time. (subject to the rod fishing rules and local fishing byelaws)

You will only need a 3 rod licence if you plan to fish with 3 rods at the same time. This may be for specialist carp fishing for example.
