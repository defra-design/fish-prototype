When fishing

An enforcement officer may ask the licence holder if they are covered by a valid fishing licence. They can show them the licence they got in the post, or show their licence email or text message on their phone.

Licence holders must follow the national and local fishing byelaws (opens in new tab).

Salmon and sea trout licence holders must submit a catch return (opens in new tab) of yearly salmon and sea trout fishing activity in England and Wales. A catch return must be submitted even if no fish are caught or no fishing activity took place.
