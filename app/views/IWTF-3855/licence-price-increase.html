Changes to rod fishing licence costs

The cost of a rod fishing licence is increasing to provide vital funds to improve fisheries in England and Wales. The amount you will pay is based on the licence start date (not the date the licence is bought).

Trout and coarse fish licence (up to 2 rods)
Licence length Cost until 31 March 2024 Cost from 1 April 2024 Cost from 1 April 2025
1-day £6.60 £7.10 £7.30
8-day £13.20 £14.30 £14.70
12-month £33 £35.80 £36.80
12-month - senior or disabled £22 £23.80 £24.50
