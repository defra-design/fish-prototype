Payment reminder: your next 12 month fishing licence


Dear Sam Fisher,

Payment for your next 12 month fishing licence will be taken from your card ending in 4321 in one month's time.

You do not need to do anything unless your circumstances have changed.

Your next licence

Start date: 11pm, 30 June 2024

End date: 10:59pm, 30 June 2025

Price: £35.80

Licence type: trout and coarse (2 rods)

Length: 12 months

You will get your new licence by email after payment has been taken.

Need to cancel the automatic payment?

You can cancel your payment at https://www.gov.uk/example-cancel-payment-link

Need to make a change?

Update your contact details

If your contact details have changed, email us at rodlicencing@environment-agency.gov.uk or call us on 03708 506 506 (between 8am and 6pm, Monday to Friday). Calls are charged at the local rate.

Change your licence type

You need to cancel the automatic payment and buy a new licence if you want to:

  • change the number of rods or type of fish covered by the licence
  • buy a discounted licence because of a disability

You can cancel the automatic payment at https://www.gov.uk/example-cancel-payment-link

Change your payment method

If you'd like to pay with a different debit or credit card, you need to cancel the automatic payment and buy a new licence at https://www.gov.uk/example-cancel-payment-link

Buy an additional licence

You need to buy an additional licence if you want to fish with 4 rods. You can buy an additional licence at https://www.gov.uk/fishing-licences/buy-a-fishing-licence

Thank you,
The Environment Agency Fisheries Team
