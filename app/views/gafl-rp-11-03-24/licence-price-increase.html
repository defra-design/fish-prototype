Changes to rod fishing licence prices

The price of a rod fishing licence is increasing over 3 years from 1 April 2023 to provide vital funds to improve fisheries in England and Wales. Price changes will take effect based on the licence start date.

The age licence holders can get a discounted 12-month licence is also changing from age 65 to age 66 and over from 1 April 2023.
