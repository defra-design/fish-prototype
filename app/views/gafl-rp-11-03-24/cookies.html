Cookies

When you use the 'Get a rod fishing licence' service, we put small files known as cookies onto your computer.

These cookies are used to:

  • remember what notifications you’ve seen so that you’re not shown them more than once
  • to protect the service against malicious activity
  • to ensure that you have a consistent experience when we make changes to the service

Find out how to manage cookies on the Information Commissioners’ website (opens in new tab).

Session and security cookies

We store session cookies on your computer to help keep your information secure while you use the service.

Session cookie details
Name Purpose Expires
__Host-Sid Used for session management 24 hours after you stop using the service
__Host-CsrfToken Used to protect the service against malicious activity Until you close your browser

Our introductory message

You may see a pop-up welcome message when you first visit the service. We’ll store a cookie so that your computer knows you’ve seen it and knows not to show it again.

Introductory message cookie details
Name Purpose Expires
seen_cookie_message Stores a message to let us know that you have seen our cookie message 1 month

AWSALBTGCORS Used to ensure you receive a consistent experience 7 days after you stop using the service

Analytics cookies (optional)

With your permission, we use first party cookies - Google Analytics - to collect data about how you use this service. This information helps us to improve our service. These cookies are optional. You can choose to accept or reject them.

We do not use any other optional cookies apart from Google Analytics

Google Analytics stores anonymised information about:

  • how you got to the service
  • pages you visit and how long you spend on each page
  • what you click on while you are using the service

Neither the 'Get a rod fishing licence' service not Google store any personal details, including your IP address with this information. You cannot be identified.

Google is not allowed to use or share our analytics data with anyone.
