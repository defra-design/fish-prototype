A recurring card payment is a regular debit or credit card payment taken with your agreement to pay securely for future fishing licences.

Payments

You can give consent to set up a recurring card payment when buying a 12-month fishing licence online.

Our payment service provider (Worldpay) will take payments (in pounds) until you tell us or your bank to stop them. Payments will appear as 'Get a Fishing Licence' on your bank statement.

Payments will be taken annually before your new licence is issued.

The cost of a future 12-month fishing licence may change. For example, the cost may be higher because of a service-wide price increase, or lower if you become eligible for a senior licence.

If a recurring card payment fails, we will contact you so you can arrange to buy another licence if you want to continue to fish.

Payment reminders

Before taking a recurring card payment, we will tell you the payment amount using the contact details you have provided.

If your contact details change, contact us to update your details by emailing enquiries@environment-agency.gov.uk or phoning 03708 506 506 (Monday to Friday, 8am to 6pm). Find out about call charges (opens in new tab).

Cancelling a recurring card payment

You can cancel your recurring card payment to end the payment agreement at any time. Cancelling your recurring card payment will not cancel or refund your current licence â€“ you can still fish until your current licence expires.

If you have received a refund for your licence (see the refund policy) your recurring card payment will be cancelled automatically.

If you would like to change the number of rods or type of fish covered by your next licence, you will need to cancel your recurring card payment and buy a new licence.

If you become eligible for a disabled concession, you can cancel your recurring card payment and buy your next licence at a reduced price.

Cancel online

You can cancel your recurring card payment online up to 11:59pm the day before your payment is due to be taken.

Cancel by phone or email

Phone 03708 506 506 (Monday to Friday, 8am to 6pm) up to the day before payment is due to be taken. Find out about call charges (opens in new tab).

Email enquiries@environment-agency.gov.uk up to 14 days before payment is due to be taken.

Cancel through your bank

If you cancel a recurring card payment through your bank, we may not know about this until we try to take the payment.

Data security

We will store and use your data following our privacy policy.
