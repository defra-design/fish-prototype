A recurring card payment is an automatic debit or credit card payment taken with your agreement to pay securely for your future fishing licences.

Payments

You can give consent to set up automatic card payments for future 12-month fishing licences when buying a licence online.

Our payment service provider (Worldpay) will take payments (in pounds) until you tell us or your bank to stop them. Payments will appear as 'Get a Fishing Licence' on your bank statement.

Payments will be taken annually before your new licence is issued.

The cost of a future 12-month fishing licence may change. For example, the cost may be higher because of a service-wide price increase, or lower if you become eligible for a senior licence.

If an automatic payment fails, we will contact you so you can arrange to buy another licence if you want to continue to fish.

Payment reminders

Before taking an automatic payment, we will tell you the payment amount using the contact details you have provided.

If your contact details change, contact us to update your details by emailing enquiries@environment-agency.gov.uk or phoning 03708 506 506 (Monday to Friday, 8am to 6pm, find out about call charges).

Refunds and cancellation

To request a refund within 14 days of buying a licence, see the refund policy.

To cancel automatic payments at any time, you can use the cancellation link in your confirmation or payment reminder email or text.

You can also cancel automatic payments by emailing enquiries@environment-agency.gov.uk or phoning 03708 506 506 (Monday to Friday, 8am to 6pm, find out about call charges). Contact us by phone to cancel a payment that is due to be taken in the next 14 days.

If you cancel an automatic payment through your bank, we may not know about this until we try to take the payment.

If you would like to change the number of rods or type of fish covered by your licence, you will need to cancel your automatic payment and buy a new licence.

If you become eligible for a disabled concession, you can cancel your next automatic payment and buy a new licence at a reduced price.

Data security

We will store and use your data following our privacy policy.
